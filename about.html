About Us

Space Is For Everyone

BigPlanetarium is Bristol's award winning space education centre. In our opinion, The science and wonder of the universe should be interesting, engaging, accessible and exciting for all people of all ages and backgrounds.

A school class on a field trip, or a family out on a weekend. Whether a novice or an avid astronomy fan we welcome all ages. Interactive experiences are made to spark curiosity and inspire the explorers for the next generation.

We ensure our physical and digital experiences are fully accessible to all. Everyone should have the right to thoroughly accessible platforms and not believe that science "is not for them".

Education

School Trips

Big Planetarium provides customized instructional programs tailored to the National curriculum for key stages one through five. Our workshops as well as shows are. Created to enhance space science’s luminescence, memory utility and relevance to the curriculum.

With our dedicated education staff we are resourced for up to 150 students per day. All materials are ready in accessible formats including large print and audio description.

What's Included

  • Full-dome planetarium show (40 minutes)
  • Guided exhibit tour with education officer
  • Interactive workshop (choose from 8 themes)

Relaxed Sessions

Our Relaxed Sessions adapt planetarium shows for all our visitors. Those who gain from a quieter, calmer atmosphere, including autistic visitors. People who have sensory sensitivities, as well as learners with disabilities.

Held on the first Sunday of each month at 10:00 and 11:30. Booking is required.

Feedback & Further Help

Making access easy to all is our goal. When you face it , please inform us of any obstacles at our site or on this website. We don’t ignore any message and we take your feedback on accessibility seriously.
